About Me

Dr. Stellah Osoro Kerongo is a Clinical Psychologist and researcher based in Nairobi, Kenya, with over 15 years of experience in psychological assessment, diagnosis, and treatment using multidimensional therapeutic models. She has worked with individuals, couples, families, schools, hospitals, and corporate institutions, offering counseling, psychotherapy, mediation, and tele-therapy services. Her approach integrates evidence-based therapeutic models tailored to meet the unique needs of each client. With additional training in Kenya Sign Language, she is well-equipped to support clients within the hearing-impaired community.
